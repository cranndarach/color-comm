Color Communication: More Details

Color Could mean... If someone is on this color...
Green
  • I'm open for communication.
  • I'm not very busy.
  • What I'm working on isn't very taxing.
 Go ahead and talk to them!
Yellow
  • I'm working something hard, but not urgent.
  • I'm a little busy.
  • I'm a sort of stressed.
  • I'm having some trouble talking.
 Think:
  • Have you talked to them in person before? Do you have something to say that has a specific purpose?
  • Or, have they specifically told you it's okay to talk to them when they're on yellow (are you on their yellow list)?
  • Or, did they start the conversation?
If so, go ahead and talk to them, but if the interaction seems strained remember that it's nothing personal. Otherwise, wait until they're on green, or consider sending an email or a text, or using some other modality.
Red
  •  I'm working on something urgent.
  • I didn't sleep last night.
  • I'm not a people-person right now.
  • I am not available for non-essential conversation, for any number of reasons.
  • I am nonverbal.
  • I am on the verge of a panic attack.
 Think:
  • Is it an emergency?
  • Or, are you on their red list?
  • Or, did they start the conversation?
If so, go ahead and talk to them, but take care. You may want to make the interaction as short as possible. Otherwise, wait until they're no longer on red, or consider sending an email or a text, or using some other modality.
